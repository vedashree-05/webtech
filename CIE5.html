Breaking News: SVMITM is now "AUTONOMOUS"

After a long wait, SMVITM has finally been declared autonomous! Students are excited, hoping this change will bring more up-to-date courses and flexibility in learning.

With autonomy, the institution can now design its own syllabus, conduct exams independently, and update teaching methods in line with current industry needs.

Student Achievements – Department of CSE

Our students have made us proud once again! Final year students Riya Shetty and Akash Nayak have won first place in the National Level Hackathon “TechSolve 2025” organized by NITK Surathkal. Their project on Smart Waste Segregation using AI received wide appreciation.

The management congratulated the winners for bringing laurels to SMVITM and encouraged others to participate in upcoming innovation challenges.
